            About The Brand  
          

          

          
After several years of making Hollywood look good, stylist-turned-designer Andrea Lieberman launched her namesake line in 2008. Lieberman graduated from the Parsons School of Design and spent time in Paris and Africa before opening her own shop in New York. Soon after, she found herself styling celebrities and music icons like Gwen Stefani and Jennifer Lopez. In addition to celebrity styling and designing her own collection, Lieberman also regularly consults on several jewelry and apparel lines. Her look mixes a variety of cultural influences, approachable textures, and modern references.
